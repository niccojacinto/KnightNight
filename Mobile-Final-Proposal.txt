Project Title: 
	Knight Night

Game Scenario:
	Perspective: 2D, Top-down, Tile-based
	Genre: Real-time, Rogue-like
	Controls: Tap to move one tile.

	The player traverse a dynamically generated, tile-based dungeon filled with slimes. In each floor, the player must find a key
	and used it to unlock a chest in order to proceed to the next level.

	The purpose is to to achieve a highscore by travelling as deep into the dungeon as possible while
	killing monsters and collecting items.

Game Architecture:
	The game uses LibGDX's badlogic architecture as a base.

Game Tools:
	LibGDX
	Android Studio
